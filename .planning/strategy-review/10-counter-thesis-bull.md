# Counter-Thesis: The Briefing Is Wrong and the Rally Extends

**Date:** 2026-04-06
**Scenario:** Every bearish assumption fails. SPX 6900+ by April 11.

---

## 1. Ceasefire Holds, Hormuz Reopens

Islamabad produces a phased reopening framework. Iran, hemorrhaging ~1.5M bbl/day in lost exports, takes face-saving terms. Three ships become thirty. Oil drops to $80.

## 2. PCE/CPI Print Soft

PCE (Thursday) covers February -- before the oil spike existed. Core prints 0.2-0.3% m/m, on or below consensus. CPI (Friday, March) catches only the tail end. Both land benign. "Soft landing alive" becomes the headline.

## 3. VIX Crush: 20 to 15

VIX 20 is residual war premium, not equilibrium (pre-conflict: 12-15). Risk premium unwinds to 17 by Wednesday, 15 by Friday. Every war-panic put buyer bleeds theta and vega simultaneously. Dealer gamma flips from selling weakness to buying strength.

## 4. Short Squeeze

The bearish consensus is crowded -- elevated put/call ratios, spiked short interest. Monday opens flat-to-green instead of gapping down. Puts lose value, shorts cover, covering drives prices higher, forcing more covering. Mechanical, not sentimental. SPX pushes through 6800 and the Fibonacci "ceiling" collapses. Next stop: 6900.

## 5. Fed -- Rate Cuts Return

Oil at $80, soft inflation, resolved geopolitics. The Fed's hawkish excuse evaporates. June cut probability jumps from 20% to 50%+. Bond yields drop. Cheaper capital, lower discount rates, higher multiples -- rocket fuel.

## 6. MPW's Blue Path Is the Right One

The briefing's own source gives 40% to SPX 7100+ by May. If price exceeds 7002, the "generational bear" wave count dies per Elliott rules. The blue path becomes the only path.

## 7. What This Does to the Three Strategies

**Bear put spreads:** Max loss. SPX blows through the exhaustion zone. Short strikes get tested or breached. Expire worthless.

**Thursday PCE fade (puts before data):** Destroyed. Benign data triggers relief, not a delayed crash. Puts bought at VIX 20 bleed vega as vol compresses. The "shrug then crack Monday" sequence never fires because there is nothing to shrug off.

**H&S neckline breakdown:** Pattern never completes. Right shoulder fails to form as price accelerates through 6800. Volume confirms breakout, not breakdown.

---

**Bottom Line:** The briefing requires five things to go right simultaneously. The bull case needs one or two links to break. If ceasefire holds and data is soft, the entire thesis inverts. Size accordingly.
